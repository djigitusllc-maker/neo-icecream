Product Overview

A differentiated frozen dessert platform

#neoicecream is designed as a frozen dessert, not a conventional U.S. ice cream. This supports a lower-fat, better-for-you positioning while preserving the emotional appeal of a familiar frozen treat.

Indulgent taste. Functional logic. Modern positioning.

Core Benefits

Synbiotic concept

Retail friendly

Taste & Wellness

Not a supplement. A dessert people can enjoy.

The product story is built around a simple commercial idea: a dessert that feels enjoyable, approachable, and easier to choose more often than traditional high-fat frozen treats.

The key message is not clinical. It is consumer-friendly: tasty can be healthy, and healthy can be tasty.
