About the product

What is #neoicecream?

#neoicecream is a synbiotic frozen dessert concept built on a fermented base and designed to combine enjoyable taste with functional positioning. The product brings together probiotics, prebiotics, and a familiar frozen dessert format that can scale across multiple product lines.

The project is being developed in two parallel directions: pilot-scale product validation and investor-oriented planning for future commercial production. Our goal is to create a differentiated frozen dessert brand at the intersection of indulgence, wellness, and scalable manufacturing.
