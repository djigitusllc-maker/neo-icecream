What is #neoicecream?

#neoicecream is a next-generation frozen dessert designed for modern consumers who want the pleasure of ice cream with a lighter, more balanced experience.

The product is being developed as a better-for-you frozen dessert with a lower-calorie and lower-fat direction, while keeping the taste, texture, and enjoyment people expect from ice cream.

It combines dessert enjoyment with a more thoughtful nutritional approach.

What Does Synbiotic-Inspired Mean?

Synbiotic refers to the combination of probiotic and prebiotic concepts in one product system.

#neoicecream is inspired by this food science direction and is being developed with fermented dessert logic and prebiotic ingredient support.

At the same time, the main consumer promise remains simple: a lighter, better-for-you frozen dessert experience.

Final product claims will depend on formulation, testing, and regulatory review before commercial launch.
